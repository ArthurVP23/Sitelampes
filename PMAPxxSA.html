PMAPxxSA

Prix : 650 euros
Materials : Steel, Acrylic. Available to order.
Process : Welding, assembly and gluing.
Dimensions : 320 mm x 320 x 220 mm.

PMAPxxSA is composed of light fins,
each time arranged in a different way, much like
a painter would do with watercolors,
varying compositions and tones. Its base,
this time, is made of steel. The plastic
material forming the crown is sandblasted
hence the “SA” in its name.
