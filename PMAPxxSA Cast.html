PMAPxxSA Cast

Prix : 850 euros
Materials : Aluminum, Acrylic. Available to order.
Process : Casting, assembly and gluing.
Dimensions : 320 mm x 320 x 220 mm.

PM.01APsa Cast is a lamp with a base
composed of interconnected arms joined
by a cast-aluminum connector. The top
section is made from offcuts and small
pieces of PMMA plastic (plexiglas).
Each colored piece is polished on its
inner surface to better diffuse the
light. For me, the result feels closer
to a sculpture than a lamp. Each model
has its own unique colored “crown.” When
preparing these plastic pieces, I select
colors to create a palette with endless
variations, trying to bring out a rhythm
and a distinctive blend of tones, like
a painter blending colors and paint. The
form and surface of the light sculpture
are designed to visually “fade away”,
giving it an impression of weightlessness.
Its glow creates a soft layer of color,
gently shaping the atmosphere: a small,
luminous presence in the space.
