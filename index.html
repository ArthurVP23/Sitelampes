Collection de lampes

A series of lamps exploring the interplay of light and PMMA, with other materials yet to come. Every light has its own legs…

The series of lamps that I present shows my work
on how I travel the border between function and
sculpture. Through those material experiments
and shape researches I try to make something
happening. This overview demonstrates
my “mental walking”; development
of ideas, then material, then
drawing, then casting...
This aims at
scenography
exhibitions.
I feed
those
mental
walking
with
models,
architecture,
industry,
nature,
fiction...
And it's shown by the light experiment
and with its legs.
