Le petit monstre

Prix : 750 euros
Materials : Aluminum, Acrylic. Available to order.
Process : Casting, assembly and gluing.
Dimensions : 80 mm x 140mm x 80 mm.

Le petit monstreis a light that follows my
scenographic project, made up of narrative
objects. It begins with a research into material
and reflection within the object itself.
The surface behaves like a strange skin
you can’t quite tell what material you’re
looking at anymore. That’s what interests me:
trying to blur the mental and material
perception. Its cast aluminum base
completes its “luminous accordion”…
