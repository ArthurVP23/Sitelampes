Cleo's light

Prix : 750 euros
Materials : Aluminum, Acrylic. Available to order.
Process : Casting, assembly and gluing.
Dimensions : 220 mm x 220 x 550 mm.

The plastic crown of Cleo's light have a circular
shape.It’s made of two light rings surrounding
the bulb, creating subtle variations between
polished, transparent, and frosted acrylic.
The piece is designed so that the light coming
from its core is projected onto its square top,
creating an interesting lighting effect. As
for the base, it’s cast in aluminum and
intentionally keepssome imperfections,treated
as unique features before being carefully
polished.
